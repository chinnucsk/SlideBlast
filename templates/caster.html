What is SlideBlast?

SlideBlast is a realtime, web-based presentation tool. It lets you display slides to multiple people through the web, and ensures that all attendees are viewing the same slide.

How does it work?

After uploading your slides, you will be redirected to a "Presenter Page" giving you control over your slide presentation. This page will display a link that you can share with your audience, giving you control over an instant, web-based slide conference.

Do I need an account to use SlideBlast.com?

No! SlideBlast.com does not require registration of any kind.

What file types are allowed in a .zip file?

Your .zip file can contain the following file types:
  • Images - .gif, .png, .jpg
  • Plain Text - .txt .markdown, .xml
  • Code - .erl, .cs, .cpp, .js, .java, .sh, .sql,

Does SlideBlast provide voice conferencing?

Not yet. You will need to arrange for your own voice communication. For now, we suggest telephones.
